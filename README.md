# Form & Focus
## An Information and Motivational Fitness Website

[Link to Figma Wireframe](https://www.figma.com/design/Z3wNeeq1qib73UgQCeBVuJ/Course-Project-Wireframe?node-id=0-1&p=f&t=wZn3KutctyWT6r4m-0)

[Link to GitHub Page](https://kevinn-gonzales.github.io/course-project/)

### Goal

Whether you are new to fitness or have experience working out, this website will guide you and motivate you through your fitness journey. 
We want to create a website to highlight the importance of physical activity as well as providing exercises and workouts individuals can perform regardless of experience. 
Additionally, we want to dedicate a page to provide motivational quotes and also suggest mini challenges that will help promote physical fitness and health.

### Target Audience

- Individuals who want to begin their fitness journey but don't know where to start.
- Individuals in sports.
- Individuals who may have experience working out but want to expand their knowledge in different exercises.
- Individuals who need motivation to work out.


### Sections

**Home Page (Home)**
- Navigation bar
- Website title and description
- Hero banner/image

**Exercises/Workouts (Page 2)**
- Depending on your goals or what muscle(s) you are focusing on, this page will describe the different exercises/workouts to help you meet your goals
- Categories like beginner, amateur and advanced exercises
- Will have a gif to each exercise/workout to show the user how to perform it

**Motivation (Page 3)**
- Motivational quotes/challenges to inspire the user to progress their fitness journey
- Different categories like Disciplline, Mindset, Consistency and Recovery
